I am a Lecturer at Capital Normal University. Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher at the Beijing Institute of Mathematical Sciences and Applications, working with Professors Zhengwei Liu and Jinsong Wu.

I received my PhD from Purdue University in May 2023 under the supervision of Professor Uli Walther. I was initially trained in algebraic geometry, and my research interests later evolved toward mathematical physics.

My research lies at the interface of mathematical physics and algebra, particularly in topological quantum field theory, tensor categories, and topological phases.

Research

I study mathematical structures arising in topological quantum field theory and quantum many-body systems, including quantum double models, weak Hopf symmetry, and tensor categorical methods.
